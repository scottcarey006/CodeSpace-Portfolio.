Menu Highlights

Francesinha

Our Francesinha is made with layers of steak, sausage, and ham, topped with melted cheese and covered in a rich beer-based sauce. It is served with crispy fries for a true taste of Porto.

Bacalhau à Brás

This classic dish combines shredded salted cod, onions, and thin fried potatoes with creamy scrambled egg, finished with parsley and olives.

Polvo à Lagareiro

Our octopus is roasted until tender, then served with olive oil, garlic, and crushed potatoes for a simple but bold Portuguese flavour.
